Frequently asked questions

What is Prayer Lock?

Prayer Lock is an iOS app that helps you protect your daily quiet time with God. It keeps chosen apps locked each morning until you take time to pray, so your first minutes go to God instead of a screen.

How does it block my phone until I pray?

You choose which apps you want to hold back. Prayer Lock keeps them locked when you wake up, and they only unlock once you have finished your quiet time. It gently removes the distraction so prayer comes first.

Is it free?

Yes. Prayer Lock is free to download on the App Store and free to use for building a daily quiet-time habit.

Is it on Android?

Not yet. Prayer Lock is currently available on iOS. An Android version is on the way, and you can join the waitlist to be notified when it launches.

Does it read my prayers?

No. Your prayers and reflections are private. Prayer Lock does not read them, and anything you write stays yours.

What if I miss a day?

That is okay. Prayer Lock is built on grace, not guilt. If you miss a day your streak simply restarts, and you are welcome to begin again the very next morning.
