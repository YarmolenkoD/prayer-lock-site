Questions

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about building your prayer habit with Prayer Lock.

What is Prayer Lock?

Prayer Lock is an iOS habit app that makes prayer the first win of your day. It blocks the distracting apps that derail your morning until you complete your prayer time, then tracks your progress with a daily streak.

How does it help me build a prayer habit?

It uses the habit loop of cue, routine, and reward. Reaching for a blocked app is the cue, praying is the routine, and unlocking your phone plus growing your streak is the reward. Repeated daily, this turns prayer into an automatic part of your morning.

Is it free?

Yes. Prayer Lock is free to download on the App Store and free to use for building your daily prayer habit.

Is it on Android?

Not yet. Prayer Lock is currently available on iOS only. An Android version is in the works and you can join the waitlist to be notified when it launches.

What happens if I break my streak?

You simply start again the next morning. There is no shame and no penalty. Every day is a fresh chance to rebuild momentum, and missing a day does not erase the progress you have already made.

Is this only for religious people?

Prayer Lock is built around prayer, but you define what your prayer time looks like. Whether it is scripture, meditation, gratitude, or quiet reflection, the app helps you protect that time and turn it into a lasting habit.
