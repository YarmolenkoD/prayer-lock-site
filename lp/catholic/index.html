Questions

What is Prayer Lock?

Prayer Lock is an iOS app that helps you keep your daily prayer rule. It locks distracting apps on your phone until you have prayed, so that your rosary, morning offering or examen comes before the noise of the day.

How does it block my phone until I pray?

You choose which apps to block. Those apps stay locked until you mark your prayer time as kept. Once you have prayed your rule, you unlock your phone and continue your day.

Is it free?

Yes. Prayer Lock is free to download on the App Store.

Is it on Android?

Not yet. Prayer Lock is available on iOS today. An Android version is in the works — you can join the waitlist to be told when it arrives.

Is it Catholic or Orthodox specific?

Neither and both. Prayer Lock works for any tradition. You choose your own prayers — the rosary, the Jesus Prayer, a personal prayer rule, the Liturgy of the Hours or anything else. The app simply guards the time.

Does it track the content of my prayers?

No. Prayer Lock does not read or record what you pray. It only tracks whether you kept your prayer time, so your streak and history stay private to you.
