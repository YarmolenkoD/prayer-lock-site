How it works

Three steps to a quieter phone.

No willpower marathons and no complicated settings. Just a small amount of friction placed exactly where you need it.

STEP 1

Choose apps to block

Pick the apps that pull you in — social feeds, video, games, anything you reach for on autopilot.

STEP 2

Your phone locks them

When you open a blocked app, Prayer Lock stops you at the door instead of letting you fall straight into the feed.

STEP 3

Pause, then unlock

Complete a short moment of prayer or intention to unlock. That single beat is often enough to remember why you picked up the phone.

Why it works

A blocker with a pause beats a blocker you tap through.

Distraction is usually a reflex, not a decision. Prayer Lock breaks the reflex by making the unlock a conscious act — small enough to respect, real enough to interrupt the habit.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is Prayer Lock?

Prayer Lock is a focus app for iPhone that blocks the apps that distract you and requires one short, intentional pause before you can unlock them.

How does it block apps?

You choose which apps to block, and Prayer Lock keeps them locked until you complete a brief moment of prayer or intention, which adds friction between the impulse and the scroll.

Is it free?

Yes, Prayer Lock is free to download on the App Store.

Is it on Android?

Prayer Lock is available on iOS today. An Android version is coming soon and you can join the waitlist to be notified.

How is it different from Screen Time or a normal blocker?

Most blockers can be dismissed with a single tap, but Prayer Lock replaces that instant bypass with a deliberate pause so unlocking becomes a conscious choice rather than a reflex.

Do I have to be religious to use it?

No. Prayer Lock is designed around prayer, but you decide what the unlock pause means to you, whether that is a prayer, a breath, or a moment of intention.
